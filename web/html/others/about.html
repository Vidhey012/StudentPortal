About Us

A.A.N.M & V.V.R.S.R's Student portal

This site is used by students to get their marks and attendance

This site is organized by 4 types of People

Students can view their marks as well as their attendance on "Student Portal" tab on main menu.

Administrator

Technical Head of College

Administrator is Level-1 Organizer for this site.

Administrator is responsible for giving logins to remaining organizers as well as removing them also.

Here administrator role is crucial because he maintains and manages the whole site.

The main functionality of the administrator is to register the principal,HOD's and faculty.

Principal

Head of the College

Principal is Level-2 Organizer for this site.

Principal is Limited for viewing the next-level organizers as well as their attendance also.

Principal can also view student's marks as well as their attendance also.

Principal can able to promote the students from one semester to another semester.

HOD

Head of the Department

HOD is Level-3 Organizer for this site.

HOD can able to view their respective branch's faculty list.

HOD can view their respective student's marks and attendance.

HOD will organize the whole branch's details.

HOD is responsible for taking the attendance of their faculty.

Faculty

He/She manages the class

HOD is Level-4 Organizer for this site.

Here faculty plays a crucial because student's data is entered by them.

Faculty enters student's data based on their respective pin,semester and subject.

Faculty have permission to organize the student's data itself.

The data entered by faculty can be viewed by students in the "student portal" tab on main menu.
